NYC Taxi & Limousine Commission Trip Record Data

4 types of vehicles
1. Yellow cab
- official cab that have the right to pick-up street-hailing and prearranged passengers anywhere in NYC
- each cab must have a medallion attached
- by law only 13,587 taxis/medallions available
- medallians are auctioned by the city and transferrable on the open mkt by licensed brokers

- also available through e-hail apps like Curb or Arro

2. Green cab (aka street-hail liveries)
- for-hire vehicles that are allowed to accept street-hails
- cannot accept street hails in the hail exclusionary zone (parts of manhattan and airports)
- receive permits and dispatched from licensed e-hail companies
- same equipments as yellow taxis

3. For-hire vehicle
- prearranged trips only
- Community Cars (aka Liveries), Black Cars, Luxury Limousines, and High Volume For-Hire Services

4. High volume for-hire vehicle
- at least 10,000 trips per day
- these companies are subjected to increased data reporting and driver pay requirements.
- 4 at the moment juno, lyft, uber, via (muvmi's dad)
